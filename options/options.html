How It Works

1. Click the BookmarkBrain icon in your toolbar
2. Click "Organize My Bookmarks"
3. AI analyzes and reorganizes your bookmarks into smart folders
4. Your old bookmarks are automatically backed up in "Other Bookmarks"

Terms & Privacy

Your bookmarks are sent to Google Gemini API for categorization. We don't collect or store any personal data. Backups are saved in "Other Bookmarks".
